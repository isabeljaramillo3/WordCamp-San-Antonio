About WordCamp

The beautiful city of San Antonio, TX will be the host city for WordCamp!

Explore the future of WordPress with a lineup of industry professionals. Discover new techniques to advance your career as a web designer.

Expert Speakers

Our expert speaker lineup was just announced, so don't wait too long before grabbing your tickets!

Want to meet the international WordPress community and share skills with some of the world's top experts and makers? Be the first to know what to expect for the future of WordPress.

WordCamp is committed to being inclusive and welcoming for everyone. We look forward to another intensive day of learning and sharing.

What You'll Learn

WordPress 5: New Gutenberg editor WooCommerce: E-commerce platform Hosting: Managed web hosting Themes: Creating your theme Plugins: Develop your plugin Content: Writing and podcasts

Speakers

Olivia Reynolds

Olivia Reynolds graduated from Syracuse University with her masters in Communications Design. Reynolds will be talking about how to create content and will be focusing on the writing and podcast process.

Joshua Hall

Joshua Hall graduated from Northeastern University with a degree in Business/commerce. Hall will be focusing on the commercial transactions conducted electronically on the internet.

Marcus Ledger

Marcus Ledger graduated from the University of Toronto where he studied Computer Science. Ledger will be talking about how to host a managed web.

Jennifer Hunt

Jennifer Hunt graduated from the University of Texas at Austin with a degree in Communication Studies. Hunt will be sharing the new updates of WordPress 5.0.

Mina Sharpe

Mina Sharpe graduated from Brown University with a degree in Business, Entrepreneurship, and Organizations. Sharpe will be sharing new ideas on how to create themes for different occasions.

Terry Kane

Terry Kane graduated from Belmont University where he studied Computer Science. Kane will be discussing the different types of plugins that can be used and how to develop your own.
